black beyond






black beyond is a project created to promote and uplift black creatives and radical thought. It exists in the form of an experimental livestream.
black beyond founded and curated by Jaszalyn, a black femme creative technologist.
I worked with Jaszalyn to co-create these animations using Cinema 4D & AfterEffects, which were used to promote various livestreams and workshops, as well as to maintain the vibe during the live programming.
An archive of the site, with the original animation we created can be found here.
